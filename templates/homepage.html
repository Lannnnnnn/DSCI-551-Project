Welcome to Restaurant Recommender System!


The standard recommender system can be made using many different methods: Collaborative Filtering, Alternating Least Squares, or K-Nearest Neighbors, and utilizing features such as ratings, number of reviews, categories, genres, and other data that could possibly be associated with a numerical value. However, there is a rarely used feature that could change the game: Review Text!
This recommender systenm allows user to find similiar restaurants for their interested restaurants. They could choose the city they live in and find out the restaurant information in that specific city. By analyzing the restaurant information using the TF-IDF method, the system ranks all the restaurants based on the similiarity score and their average rating score.
Finally, users could check Top N recommendation similar restaurants according to their own choice.

  • Step of Using the Restaurant Recommendation System:
    • Choose and Upload the Json Data Files
    • Choose the City interested in
    • Check the Restaurant Information and File Information
    • Choose the Restaurant Interested and Number of Recommendations
    • See the Recommendation Results
